= Language availability for the product and this guide

[role="_abstract"]
The console experience and this user guide are published for English-speaking users today. Other locales may be considered as the product matures.

Today, the {OMA} user interface and this guide are available in English. Future releases may broaden regional or language coverage; any change will be announced through normal product communication channels.
