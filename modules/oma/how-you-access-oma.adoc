= How you use {OMA} in the {HCC}

[role="_abstract"]
{OMA} is offered as a web experience after you sign in to the {HCC} with your {RH} account. The interface walks you through creating a migration assessment. Prompts, labels, and legal notices live in the product, and they are the source of truth for the exact flow in your build.

To open the service, use the {HCC} applications menu or search to find the {OMA} or migration assessment entry, then follow the on-screen steps. The service may be labeled to reflect your product bundle. Do not bookmark or share internal, nonproduction, or partner-only hosts. Use only the console that your {RH} subscription or program assigns you.

If your organization does not have access to the {HCC}, contact {RH} or an authorized partner to find out which assessment options apply to you.
